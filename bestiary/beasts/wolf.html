Wolf (Severe, +1)

Tough +4 | Nimble +6

Compentence +5 | Constitution +6

Harm 15 | Armor 1

Wolves are medium creatures weighing up to 90lbs and sizing up to 3ft tall. These creatures are pack animals with packs that average 10 wolves but ranging from 2 to 30 wolves in total. These creatures are territorial and generally stalk at night. If their pack seems to be losing the fight (half or more of them have fallen), then the pack will break and run off.

Actions

Claw (+4) - The Wolf attacks with its sharp claws.

Bite (+3) - The Wolf chomps down with its powerful jaws.

Howl - calls another Wolf to its aid. Another Wolf will join the combat on the next round if the combat has not yet ended.
