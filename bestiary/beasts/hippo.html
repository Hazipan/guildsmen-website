Hippo (T6, +0)

Tough +10 | Nimble +5

Competence +0 | Constitution +5

Harm 13 | Armor 4

Hippos are large creatures the grow up to 17ft long and 10ft tall and can weigh up to 4,000 lbs. They are highly territorial and are semi-aquatic. They spend their days in rivers and lakes and are generally found in groups of 10 to 30.

Actions

Chomp (+7) – The Hippo chomps down with its huge maw. This chomp is capable of breaking bodies and armor alike.

Drowning Shake – The Hippo grabs you with its mouth to drag you down into the water. It grapples you via an opposed Tough check. If the hippo succeeds, then you take 5 Harm and are then dragged into the water to cause 2 Harm (ignoring armor) for every round you are held under water.
