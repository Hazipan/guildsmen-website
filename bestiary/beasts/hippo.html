Hippo (Deadly, +0)

Tough +9 | Nimble +3

Compentence +2 | Constitution +8

Harm 20 | Armor 3

Hippos are large creatures the grow up to 17ft long and 10ft tall and can weigh up to 4,000 lbs. They are highly territorial and are semi-aquatic. They spend their days in rivers and lakes and are generally found in groups of 10 to 30.

Actions

Chomp (+6) - The Hippo chomps down with its huge maw. This chomp is capable of breaking bodies and armor alike.

Drowning Shake - (Requires that a body of water is nearby.)The Hippo grabs you with its mouth to drag you down into the water. It grapples you via an opposed Tough check. If the hippo succeeds, then you take 5 Harm and are then dragged into the water to cause 2 Harm (ignoring armor) for every round you are held under water.
