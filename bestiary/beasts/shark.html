Shark (Deadly, +1)

Tough +5 | Nimble +9

Compentence +4 | Constitution +7

Harm 20 | Armor 2

Sharks are large creatures that grow up to 20ft long and weigh up to 5,000lbs. Sharks generally hunt alone and usually won't attack unless provoked or hungry.

Actions

Bite (+7) - The Shark swims at you and bites down to eat you.

Ram (+4) - The Shark swims at you to ram into and Stun you for 10 seconds (1 combat round).
