Lion (Deadly, +1)

Tough +5 | Nimble +9

Compentence +5 | Constitution +8

Harm 18 | Armor 2

Lions are medium creatures that grow up to 6ft long and 4ft tall and weigh up to 550lbs. They are generally active during dawn and dusk. They generally hunt smaller prey on their own but will form groups to take down larger targets.

Actions

Claw (+7) - The Lion pounces with its sharp claws to shred you to pieces.

Bite (+6) - The Lion lashes out to take a bite of you.

Roar - The Lion bellows out a powerful roar, Frightening all creatures within 20ft for as long as they are in combat with it.
