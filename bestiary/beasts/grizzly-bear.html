Grizzly Bear (Dealdy, +0)

Tough +9 | Nimble +4

Compentence +1 | Constitution +7

Harm 18 | Armor 3

Grizzly bears are medium creatures weighing up to 1,500lbs and sizing up to 8.5ft tall. They travel on their own, and are extremely territorial and are generally active during the day.

Actions

Claw (+6) - The Grizzly Bear comes at you with its thick claws to maim your body.

Bite (+7) - The Grizzly Bear goes to take a huge bite out of you.

Maul (+9) - The Grizzly Bear furiously uses teeth and claws to decimate you.

Roar - The Grizzly Bear bellows out a powerful roar, Frightening all creatures within 20ft for as long as they are in combat with it.
