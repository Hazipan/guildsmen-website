Dire Bear (T6, +1)

Tough +12 | Nimble +7

Competence +0 | Constitution +5

Harm 14 | Armor 4

Dire bears are large creatures weighing up to 15,000lbs and sizing up to 10ft tall while on all four legs. These creatures travel on their own. Dire Bears are extremely territorial and are generally active during the day.

Actions

Claw (+5) – The Dire Bear comes at you with its thick claws to maim your body.

Bite (+6) – The Dire Bear goes to take a huge bite out of you.

Maul (+8) – The Dire Bear furiously uses teeth and claws to decimate you.

Roar – The Dire Bear bellows out a powerful roar, Frightening all creatures within 20ft for as long as they are in combat with it.
