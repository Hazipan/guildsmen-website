Dire Wolf (T6, +1)

Tough +10 | Nimble +12

Competence +5 | Constitution +4

Harm 13 | Armor 4

Dire Wolves are medium creatures weighing up to 200lbs and sizing up to 6ft tall. These creatures are seen as the alpha of any wolf pack. So long as the Dire Wolf fights, all the wolves in its pack will continue to fight as well.

Actions

Claw (+9) – The Dire Wolf attacks with its sharp claws.

Bite (+7) – The Dire Wolf chomps down with its powerful jaws.

Howl – calls a common wolf to its aid. Another wolf will join the combat on the next round if the combat has not yet ended.

Growl – causes all non-wolf creatures within 50ft to become Frightened for as long as they are in combat with it.
