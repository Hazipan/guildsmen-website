Mountain Lion (Severe, +1)

Tough +4 | Nimble +6

Compentence +3 | Constitution +6

Harm 15 | Armor 2

Mountain Lions (also known as Cougars) are medium creatures that grow up to 3ft tall and 8ft long and weigh up to 150lbs. Cougars are territorial and generally hunt alone, at night.

Actions

Claw (+5) - The Mountain Lion pounces with its sharp claws to shred you to pieces.

Bite (+4) - The Mountain Lion lashes out to take a bite of you
